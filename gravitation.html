General Relativity

Einstein's Spacetime Fabric

Newton said gravity is a force. Einstein revealed it's a geometry: mass warps the fabric of spacetime, and nearby objects simply follow the curves — like a marble rolling into a dent on a rubber sheet.

Drag the heavy mass around. The grid warps around it, and the small orbiting mass follows the curvature — not a "pull".
